TSE GROWTH · 4058 Cloud Business SaaS · Anpi · kintone Integration · NotePM

Toyokumo, Inc.

トヨクモ株式会社

Toyokumo is a three-pillar Japanese SMB SaaS roll-up now priced for skepticism: the market has stopped capitalizing NotePM cross-sell before the evidence is disclosed.

At ¥1,943, the shares are 49% below the Aug-2025 peak, when Project Mode / NotePM was valued as if already plugged into Toyokumo’s kintone-led distribution engine. The May 14 Q1 reset — OP +80.5%, ad spend -14.5% YoY, 43.2% margin — shows operating leverage remains; the multiple now needs proof. Per-product OPM, a formal net-cash return policy, and a Project Mode payback bridge could re-rate 8.8x EV/EBIT without higher earnings.

TSE PRIME · 4776 Groupware & No-Code Business-App SaaS

Cybozu, Inc.

サイボウズ株式会社

Cybozu's kintone engine still compounds. FY25's profit jump was a one-time price revision and investors want it decomposed.

Group operating profit grew 106% in FY25 on a kintone ARPA step from ¥34,100 to ¥47,100, FY26 guidance reset to +4%, and the stock fell 53% from August. The May 14 ¥3.0bn buyback — Cybozu's largest authorization since the 2000 listing — and a 25% dividend hike began the answer. Three disclosures could change how the market values a 68% ROCE business at 8.9x forward EV/EBIT: ARPA decomposition, a US-subsidiary payback milestone, and the buyback restated as a policy.
