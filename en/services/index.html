Core service

Global IR Diagnosis

The IR Diagnosis Card is JII's proprietary diagnostic tool. It identifies where English disclosure, IR materials, IR websites, and investor meetings may fail to communicate clearly to global investors. The output is not just a score. It organizes priority issues, concrete examples, and practical next actions. See the diagnostic detail page.

"Before translation, what is not reaching foreign investors?"

We check capital allocation, differentiation logic, and growth assumptions: the points that drive investment decisions. We check whether they reach investors in the right order and with the right level of detail, then show fix priority.

Information handling

Confidentiality and operating practice

All work is run on the assumption that the materials contain non-public information from a listed company. The points below describe the practice applied.

We sign an NDA for every assignment. You can use our template or yours. We log involved parties, material scope, and timing internally.

Materials with non-public material information are shared only after NDA signing. At inquiry stage, a short assignment summary is enough. After receipt, we define access rights and the retention period (90 days by default) and manage accordingly.

No generative AI is used in the production translation step.

If generative AI is used for support tasks like term-base maintenance or internal checks, data is not used for training and is not sent to third parties. For highly sensitive work, we can run the assignment with no AI use.

Translation, interpretation, and diagnostic work are handled directly by founder Teddy Okuyama. We do not outsource. In peak periods like earnings season, we manage accepted volume to protect deadlines and quality.

Personal information is handled under our privacy policy. Source materials and deliverables are deleted 90 days after delivery unless otherwise agreed.
