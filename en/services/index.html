What's different about JII

Build company English, not project English.

When vendors change, terminology changes. When documents are translated in isolation, the investment story fragments. JII reduces that rework by centralizing the term base, translation memory, and checking process. AI is used as production support: to find inconsistencies, compare against past wording, and flag issues before delivery.

Track B — The retainer's central deliverable

The Foreign IR Diagnostic Card

Clean English does not automatically move an investment decision. The Diagnostic Card is a one-page internal tool that scores 12 items, identifies where foreign investors stop, and names the next fixes to make.

See retainer pricingDiscuss the diagnostic

The card shows, item by item, where foreign investors stop and what they still cannot underwrite.

It is not a style critique. It is the priority list that keeps translation, review, Q&A, and meeting preparation moving in the same direction.

  • 12-item, 24-point scorecard
  • Top 3 communication gaps
  • Specific evidence from your IR materials and observed meetings
  • Recommended next fix — what to change first
  • Built for IR / CFO office / Corporate Planning

Track A vs. Track B

One-off delivers the asset. Retainer improves the cycle.

Track A finishes the current document or meeting. Track B keeps the same issues from returning every quarter by updating priorities and feeding them back into disclosure and meetings.

Track A — Standalone answers

"Translate this document, accurately and consistently — now."

Earnings releases, integrated reports, timely disclosures, and interpretation are delivered document-by-document or meeting-by-meeting. The terminology still carries forward.

Track B — Recurring answers

"Maintain English IR quality across documents and across years."

On a quarterly or annual cadence, we test whether capital allocation, competitive advantage, and growth assumptions are landing. The Diagnostic Card identifies the fixes; the retainer pushes them into the next cycle.
