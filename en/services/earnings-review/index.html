Service 04 / Earnings Presentation Review

Shape the earnings deck
around how foreign investors will read it.

We review the English earnings summary, earnings presentation deck, earnings-call script, and supporting materials from the foreign-investor side. The recurring points — KPI consistency, main messages, degree of certainty on forward guidance, and expected Q&A — are tightened each quarter, so the earnings call lands more clearly with investors over time.

Review scope

What is reviewed

01

Earnings summary (English)

From the summary block to performance highlights, segments, and forward guidance — reviewed as one set.

02

Earnings presentation (English)

Per-slide main messages, KPI consistency, and capital-allocation narrative.

03

Earnings-call script

CEO / CFO opening remarks and expected Q&A, reviewed from the investor side.

04

Supplementary materials / fact book

Segment detail, KPI history, capex breakdown and other English supplementary materials.

05

Expected Q&A

Likely foreign-investor questions and recommended answer outlines, organised by investor frame.

06

Quarterly cadence (optional)

Ongoing review aligned to the quarterly earnings cycle.

Deliverables

Deliverables

  • Comment-marked review: comments inserted directly into the earnings materials, presentation, and script, with priorities organised.
  • Main-message list: three to five points to land with investors, each with the supporting evidence.
  • Expected Q&A list: 20–40 questions by investor frame, with answer outlines.
  • 30–60 minute readout, in person or online: walk-through of the findings.
FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can you also produce the translation?
As a rule, the translation itself stays with your existing translation partner; JII engages on the review side. This is so we remain an independent adviser rather than a translation vendor.
When in the earnings cycle should we engage you?
Ideally, when the English draft is ready, 5–7 business days before the announcement. Same-day support is offered separately as earnings-call interpretation.
Are ongoing engagements possible?
Yes. Quarterly ongoing support (four cycles a year) is covered under Foreign investor IR support.
How long does it take?
4–7 business days, depending on volume. If the announcement date is close, please raise it in advance.
