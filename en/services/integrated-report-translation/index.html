Integrated Report Translation

We support integrated report translation for overseas investor communication.

Discuss this service

Who this is for

  • IR and disclosure teams preparing overseas-facing communication
  • Companies needing investor-ready English for documents or meetings
  • Teams that want pre-disclosure or pre-meeting checks

What JII supports

  • Document or meeting-specific support
  • Investor-facing wording and logic adjustments
  • Terminology and consistency checks

Common issues

  • Literal translation weakens the core message
  • Key investor concerns are not addressed clearly
  • Tight timelines reduce quality control

JII’s approach

We treat translation and interpretation as investor communication design, not only language conversion.

Deliverables

  • Service execution for target documents/meetings
  • Adjustment comments
  • Final pre-release/pre-meeting check as needed

Optional overseas investor diagnostic layer

Where useful, we can also review how the material or meeting communication is likely to appear to overseas investors.

Process

  1. Confirm materials and schedule
  2. Define scope
  3. Execute translation / interpretation / diagnostics
  4. Deliver and review
