Equity Story Design

Equity story design — carry post-diagnosis findings into English disclosure, IR materials, the IR website, and investor meetings as one story.

Issues that surface in global IR diagnosis often can't be resolved by fixing wording in part of a document. Starting from the diagnosis card and the premise agreed in the internal walkthrough, we organize investment highlights, growth premise, profitability, capital allocation, and competitive advantage into a single investor-facing equity story. We design the spine so the same logic can be told repeatedly across English disclosure, IR materials, the IR website, and meeting Q&A.
