Dedicated IR Support

Dedicated IR Support — keep the improvement that started with global IR diagnosis going quarter after quarter, building English IR that reaches overseas investors.

Overseas IR support rarely sticks after a single English disclosure or review. The point is not to re-explain the company from scratch each time, but to improve materials, meetings, interpretation, and review as one flow with a partner who understands the business background and the equity story. Our Dedicated IR Support maintains the foundation built through global IR diagnosis, the IR diagnosis card, the internal walkthrough, equity story design, quarterly disclosure improvement, and meeting prep & Q&A — and continues to support the English IR and investor dialogue improvement loop.
