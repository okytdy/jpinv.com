Earnings Presentation Translation

Earnings presentation translation for Japan-listed issuers: management-message clarity, KPI consistency, and investor-facing English aligned with your disclosure set.

Discuss this service

Japanese counterpart: 日本語ページを見る

Primary use case

Translation for 決算説明会資料 used in quarterly results briefings and overseas follow-up distribution.

Where quality breaks

  • Slides and script use different terminology for the same KPI.
  • Strategy narrative is grammatically correct but intent is diluted.
  • Risk factors and assumptions disappear from English summaries.

JII approach

Founder-led review across deck flow, term-base continuity with prior disclosures, protected handling of confidential information, and no generative AI in production translation (AI-free option available).

Outputs

  • Slide-by-slide English text optimized for investor reading speed.
  • Terminology harmonization with earnings summary and timely disclosures.
  • Final consistency check pack before publication.
