IR Diagnosis Card

The IR Diagnosis Card turns global IR diagnosis into the next improvement decision.

The IR Diagnosis Card is a practical handover that converts global IR diagnosis from impression into a structured set of issues. We look across English disclosure, the IR website, and investor meetings to identify where overseas investor understanding stalls — and capture it as 12 items / 24 points. It's not a score for its own sake. It's a format IR, the CFO office, corporate planning, and management can use to agree on what to fix in the next quarter.
